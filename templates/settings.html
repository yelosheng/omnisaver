Twitter / X Cookie (xreach)

Enables logged-in scraping via xreach — access more content and avoid login walls. Export cookies from Cookie-Editor (Chrome extension) as JSON while logged in to x.com. Use a secondary account to reduce risk.

XiaoHongShu Cookie Refresh

Reddit Cookie

Zhihu (知乎) Cookie

Required to bypass login walls and anti-bot for Zhihu answers and articles. Export all cookies from Cookie-Editor while logged in to zhihu.com, then paste the JSON here.
