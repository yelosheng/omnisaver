Configure Backend URL

The script defaults to http://localhost:6201. To change it:

  1. Click the Tampermonkey icon → find the script
  2. Click ⚙️ 设置后端地址
  3. Enter your address (e.g. http://192.168.1.x:6201) and refresh the page
Toast Notifications
  • Green — Saved successfully
  • Yellow — Already exists in archive
  • Red — Failed — check the service is running or cookies are valid
  • Blue — Request in progress
